PoU is a full node incentive initiative that allows users running a node to either earn rewards with no existing balance, or stake their holdings to earn additional BTH. A network can never have too many nodes and Bithereum aims to boast one of the strongest full node networks in all of crypto.

TOP TO BOTTOM INCENTIVES

The Bithereum Network rewards every participant in the network. The ultimate vision of the network is fully inclusive and incentivized: from miners, to full nodes, to holders who wish to stake, every member of the community has the ability to earn incentives and be an essential part of keeping the network alive and secure.
